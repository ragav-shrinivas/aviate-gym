Legal

Refund
Policy

We want you fully confident in your decision to train with us. Here's exactly how refunds, cancellations, and your free trial work.

Last Updated: 9 June 2026

This Refund Policy explains the circumstances under which Gokul Srinivasan's Aviate Gym ("Aviate Gym", "we", "us", or "our") offers refunds for memberships, in-person sessions, and online coaching programs. By enrolling in any paid program, you agree to the terms set out below. This policy should be read together with our Terms & Conditions.

01

Our Approach

Coaching is a personal, time-intensive service. Once a program begins, our coaches dedicate significant time to designing your plan, reviewing your progress, and supporting you. Because of this, refunds are considered on a fair, case-by-case basis within the framework below.

02

Free Trial Session

Your first in-person session is complimentary and carries no charge, so no refund applies. It exists precisely so you can experience Aviate before committing to any paid program.

03

In-Person Memberships & Sessions

  • Before activation: If you cancel a paid membership before your first paid session and before any plan has been prepared, you are eligible for a full refund.
  • After activation: Once your membership is active, refunds are calculated on a pro-rata basis for the unused, remaining period, less any sessions already attended and an administrative fee where applicable.
  • Personal training packages: Unused, pre-paid sessions may be refunded pro-rata; sessions already completed are non-refundable.
04

Online Coaching Programs

  • Cooling-off window: You may request a full refund within 7 days of payment, provided your custom plan has not yet been delivered and coaching has not commenced.
  • After your plan is delivered: Once your personalised plan, nutrition guidance, or first check-in has been provided, the program is considered active and is generally non-refundable, as the core service has been rendered.
  • Mid-program: In exceptional circumstances (such as a documented medical condition preventing participation), we may offer a partial refund or program pause at our discretion.
05

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for a refund:

  • Sessions or coaching periods already completed or delivered.
  • Programs cancelled due to a client's failure to participate, follow the plan, or respond to check-ins.
  • Promotional, discounted, or specially-priced enrolments where marked non-refundable at the time of purchase.
  • Any registration or administrative fees, where applicable.
06

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us on WhatsApp at +91 97898 92696 with your name, the program you enrolled in, your date of payment, and the reason for your request. We will review your request and respond, typically within 3–5 business days.

07

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take 7–14 business days for the amount to reflect in your account after approval.

08

Rescheduling & Cancellations

We understand life happens. Sessions may be rescheduled with reasonable advance notice at no charge. Repeated late cancellations or no-shows may, however, result in the forfeiture of the affected session.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the most recent version. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase governs that purchase.

This Refund Policy is provided for general informational purposes and does not constitute legal advice. We recommend reviewing it with a qualified professional to ensure it aligns with applicable consumer-protection laws in your jurisdiction.
