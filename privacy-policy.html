Privacy
Policy

Your trust matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we keep it safe.

Last Updated: 9 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gokul Srinivasan's Aviate Gym ("Aviate Gym", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you visit our website, book a session, enrol in our in-person or online coaching programs, or otherwise interact with us. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described below.

01

Information We Collect

We only collect information that helps us serve you better. Depending on how you interact with us, this may include:

  • Contact details — your name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, city, and country.
  • Health & fitness information — age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, experience level, dietary preferences, and any injuries or medical conditions you choose to share.
  • Photographs — progress or current photos you voluntarily upload as part of an online coaching intake.
  • Booking details — preferred session dates, program interest, and any notes you provide.
  • Technical data — basic browser and device information collected automatically when you visit the website.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to deliver and improve our services, including to:

  • Confirm and schedule your free session or coaching enrolment.
  • Design personalised training and nutrition programs around your goals and biology.
  • Communicate with you about your program, progress, and check-ins — primarily over WhatsApp, call, or email.
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Maintain internal records and improve our programs and website.
03

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited information only with our own coaching staff who need it to serve you, and with trusted service providers (such as payment processors or messaging platforms) strictly to operate our services. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect our legal rights.

04

WhatsApp & Third-Party Communication

Our intake and booking forms are designed to send your details directly to our coaching team via WhatsApp. When you submit a form, a copy may also be downloaded to your own device for your records. Communication through WhatsApp is additionally governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy, which we encourage you to review.

05

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

06

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services, maintain coaching records, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. When information is no longer needed, we take steps to delete or anonymise it.

07

Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, simply contact us using the details below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

08

Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use minimal cookies or similar technologies to ensure the site functions correctly and to understand how visitors use it. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended if you do.

09

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 14 and above, and minors should only enrol with the consent and supervision of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when it was most recently revised. We encourage you to review it periodically.

This Privacy Policy is provided for general informational purposes and reflects our current practices. It is not legal advice. We recommend reviewing it with a qualified professional to ensure compliance with applicable laws in your jurisdiction.
