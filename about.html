How We
Got Here.

Aviate didn't happen overnight. It was built rep by rep, year by year, member by member — driven by one man's obsession with creating something that actually works.

2012

The Beginning

Gokul Srinivasan opens the first Aviate facility in Chennai with a simple promise: no shortcuts, no gimmicks, just results. The first 50 members see what world-class coaching looks like.

2015

The Programs Take Shape

After hundreds of client transformations and thousands of data points, Aviate's signature programs are formalised — Strength & Conditioning, HIIT, Mobility, and Personal Training — each refined to a science.

2018

1,000 Lives Changed

A milestone that validates everything. A thousand men and women walk out of Aviate fundamentally different — stronger, leaner, more confident, more capable. The word spreads.

2020

Online Coaching Launches

Distance is no longer a barrier. Aviate's first online coaching programs launch, bringing precision programming to clients across India and internationally.

2022

Specialised Women's Programs

Recognising the unique needs of women's physiology, Aviate launches dedicated programs for PCOD, thyroid conditions, and female-specific fat loss — addressing the root cause, not just symptoms.

2024

3,000+ & Still Climbing

Over three thousand transformations. A community of warriors across Chennai and beyond. An AI-powered coaching system making world-class programming accessible to anyone, anywhere.

The Founder

Gokul
Srinivasan

Founder & Head Coach — Aviate Gym

Gokul Srinivasan didn't set out to build a gym. He set out to build something that actually worked. After years of seeing people drift through cookie-cutter fitness programs with mediocre results, he built Aviate around a single idea: that exceptional coaching — not equipment or gimmicks — is the variable that changes everything.

Over twelve years, Gokul has personally coached thousands of clients — from complete beginners to competitive athletes — and has developed specialised programs for body recomposition, hormonal fat loss, lean gaining, and more. His approach combines sports science with genuine human connection.

Today, Gokul leads a team of elite coaches at Aviate and continues to personally oversee every online coaching client — because for him, your result is personal.
